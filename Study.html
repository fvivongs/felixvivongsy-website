Are you an engineering student with no money and no textbooks? Don't worry! Below is a list of textbooks that are archived in a Google Drive folder. These cover a few of the rudimentary engineering classes with all the essential resources you need to be successful in engineering. 

  • Mechanics of Materials, 4th Edition, Timothy A. Philpot
  • Fundamentals of Machine Component Design, 6th Edition, Robert C. Juvinall
  • Fundamentals of Machine Component Design, 5th Edition, Robert C. Juvinall
  • Incompresible Flow, 4th Edition, Ronald L. Panton
  • Modern Compressible Flow, 3th Edition, John D. Anderson
  • Applied Numerical Methods with MatLab, 4th Edition, Steven C. Chapra
  • Introduction to Heat Transfer, 6th Edition, Theodore L. Bergman
  • Machinery's Handbook, 30th Edition, Christopher J. McCauley
  • Materials Science and Engineering, 9th Edition, William D. Callister
  • Fundamentals of Engineering Thermodynamics, 8th Edition, Wiley
  • Engineering Mechanics Dynamics, 8th Edition, Wiley
  • Experimental Methods for Engineers, 8th Edition, J.P. Holman
