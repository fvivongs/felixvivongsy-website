 VIEW MY RESUME

At Duke Energy I worked as a reliability engineer. As simple as generating and distributing power may seem, there is a lot that goes into the industry, that we take for granted! I discovered that numerous outages are recorded and processed before being submitted to a database. This was a tedious task that required a lot of man hours, so I designed a tool, using Python, that allowed users to upload data packed spreadsheets and recieve feedback (within seconds) on potential errors. 

During the summer of 2017 & 2018 I interned at Optum. I worked as both a SCRUM Lead and UI/UX Software Developer. 

My first Internship was back in 2016 at a civil engineering consulting firm, HNTB.
